About

Bella Paper Design is a full service design studio. We are your creative partner. Bella Paper Design is passionate and experienced about graphic design. Our clients become part of the design team. We believe design can create positive and informative connections. Founded in 2001 in the Bay Area, Bella Paper Design is now located 20 miles south of Salt Lake City.

Bella Paper Design's philosophy is great communication between client and designer can lead to a successful project. Clear deadlines and milestones keep projects on task and successful. Open communication between designer and client is key to successful design. Good design communicates, solves a problem, connects and leaves a lasting impression. Bella Paper Design's focus is on beautiful typography, great color schemes, clear messaging and beautiful imagery combined to uniquely identify and brand a product, service or an idea.
