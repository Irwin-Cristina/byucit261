Bella Paper Design Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a portfolio of design work, contact information, blog, service, contact and resume for Bella Paper Design.

Target Audience

My target audience's age range is from 20-70 years old. Basically anyone who would want graphic design services. My site will appeal to both men and women in the United States living in a city or in a rural area. The average income would be between $50,000 and $200,000 and my target audience would have at least graduated from high school. My target audience will be single or married. They will use the web often to do research and they will use both mobile devices and computers.

Typography

I chose a sans-serif font and a serif font because they are contrasting. I used the sans-serif for the headings and the serif for the body copy. Both are elegant fonts that will contrast each other well.
